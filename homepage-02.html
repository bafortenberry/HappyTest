Why consider a happiness major?

David Foster Wallace tells a story about two fish swimming along.

An older fish swims by and asks,
“How’s the water today?”.

One young fish looks at the other and asks,
“what the hell is water?”.

This story is meant to show how we often miss what’s right in front of us.
The happiness major (a Bachelor's degree created through the University of Alabama’s New College) is the first and only degree in happiness. Just like the fish in their ignorance of water, our society has largely overlooked a subject which is the most important part of life.
We study business, sciences, art, technology, and more – we really study everything but happiness. So why not focus on what really matters? ​

Would you like to learn how to be more productive, how to train your mind like a muscle, or figure out what to do with your life? Whether your question is big or small, this site will help.
